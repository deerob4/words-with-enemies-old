Instructions

Welcome to Words With Enemies! Your job is to defend the world from the ongoing Ooglie invasion. Each turn, you will be given a selection of letters - use them to make the biggest word you can! The Ooglies will do the same, and at the end of the round the words will be fired at each other.

Letters that are the same in both words will collide and explode, so choose carefully! The winner of each round is the one who has the most letters left over. For example, if you chose "quartette", and the Ooglies chose "fairs", you would win, because the Ooglies would have "fis" left over and you would have "qutette". It makes sense when you play!
